Name England
General location Part of a small island off the West coast of Europe.
Description A strange place, where the locals drink warm beer and eat boring food, including boiled vegetables.

Originally occupied by the Brttons, Romans, Angles, Saxons, and other barbarians. Later they were invaded by the Vikings and the Normans. Tired of being conquered, the English developed a very strong navy and set about conquering the rest of the world, calling their collection of colonies an "Empire". They have spent the last thousand years focussed on the task of annoying the French, a role recently taken over by Donald Trump.

The English claim that they have not been invaded successfully for twelve hundred years, but the Pakistanis know better.
